About Swoosh

Swoosh is a compact tennis tracking system that keeps your phone's camera locked on the ball, using an ESP32‑powered servo mount and an on‑device lightweight AI model. It helps players and coaches focus on technique—not on framing.

How it works

  • Computer vision detects and tracks ball movement in real time.
  • Servo mount adjusts the phone angle to keep the action centered.
  • Analytics help you measure progress over time.

Why Swoosh?

Record cleaner footage, reduce setup time, and capture every rally. No bulky tripods or complex gear—just your phone and Swoosh.
